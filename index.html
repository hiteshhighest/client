A testimony to the art of sword fighting.
Dragonborn is a foundational sword clan that upholds its existing prevalence among the sword world and strives to fulfill the common desire of improvement in all of its members and grant a sense of belonging. We are Dragonborn, housing of the most prestige and elite sword fighters. As one of ROBLOX's most dominant sword fighting clans, we defeat all who challenge us. Join us and be part of a legacy that celebrates unwavering dedication and guarantees unmatched strength.

Community
